Links to other interesting simulations

For driving companys

Our first goal was not writing a simulation, we wished to show buses (or something similar) in realtime on a map to have the possibility to see were the bus is currently. If you are interested in having something like this please write us. We will try to make at least work for you. We just need the GPS data.

For optimizing traffic flow

For this cause we recommend a simulation far better compared to us. You can use a map of your city and modify it. If you have data about the traffic flow in your city you can even simulate this. Should you have problems with this simulation refer to the side of the developers or, if you like, at us directly.

For people how just want to play arround with traffic

We recommend the simulation of Martin Treiber we also use his equations in our simulation. There you can change certain parameter e.g. the politness or set traffic lights and observe the traffic.
