The traffic simulation

This model simulates in realtime the flow of traffic

The traffic model

Directly above this paragraph you can see the simulated cars. They are beeing calculated through a differential equation of Martin Treiber which he uses even more impressive. Our advantage is we show the results there where you need them: In the city. Presented on a map. With this option we want to make it possible to optimize a town according to the traffic, currently we recommend looking at this page. There one can get background information and links towards other simulations

What do we simulate?

We simulate a amount of cars which then, without a specified play, drive through the given map. They accelerate, slow down and change directions. They do not drive aboth each other. But we are sorry. We can not simulate crossings and multiple lanes accurate yet.
